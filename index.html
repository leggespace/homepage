About the company

Why choose LeggeSpace?

With almost 10 years of experience within the Swedish IT sector, I have found that almost all problems which might not be solvable today, are solvable tomorrow. Working for startups, non-profit organizations, small established businesses as well as larger enterprises has taught me the valuable lesson of adapting a solution to the size of the client as well as the existing infrastructure. Every project comes with it's own limitations, which do not have to be seen as restrictions but instead possibilities to find solutions for each business need. I always provide personal support by phone and mail, so that if anything ever would go wrong you can be confident that such issues get fixed before they cause any business disruptions.

My and the company's core Values

Reliability, tools and systems which work today should work tomorrow. When they don't, they should explain why and tell you to fix it. Time is better spent on development than constantly tracking bugs and crashes which provide incorrect information to the user or developer.

Safety and security, protecting people and data is more important than half baked features. It's therefore better to build and iterate upon a stable and secure base and add features with limited blast radius, than hoping that no breach will ever be attempted.

Life long learning, people do their best work when faced with challenges and learn from their work, not being challenged by their tools. The problem is that many of us today spend hours upon hours on easily automated manual processes, which are both draining and only provide marginal value.
